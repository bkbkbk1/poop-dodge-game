Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Poop Dodge Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Poop Dodge mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your Solana wallet (such as Phantom), we store your public wallet address locally on your device to enable token rewards functionality.
  • Game Data: High scores and coin counts are stored locally on your device.

Information Usage

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Process token reward claims on the Solana blockchain
  • Save your game progress locally

Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal information on external servers. Blockchain transactions are public by nature of the Solana network.

Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with:

  • Solana Blockchain: For processing token transactions
  • Phantom Wallet: For wallet connectivity (governed by Phantom's privacy policy)

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
